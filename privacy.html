TravelPal – Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 2026

1. What we collect

We access your email inbox solely to find travel booking confirmations (flights, hotels, restaurants, activities). We read email subjects and bodies to extract booking details. We do not store your emails — only the structured booking data we extract from them.

2. How we use your data

Extracted booking information (dates, locations, confirmation numbers, costs) is stored in your personal account and used only to build and display your travel itinerary.

3. Third-party services

We use Google Gmail API and Microsoft Graph API to read your emails, and Anthropic Claude to parse booking details. Each is governed by their own privacy policies. We use Supabase to securely store your itinerary data.

4. Data retention

Your itinerary data is retained until you delete a trip or your account. Email access tokens are stored securely on your device and are never transmitted to our servers.

5. Your rights

You may revoke email access at any time by signing out or revoking permissions in your Google or Microsoft account settings. Contact us to request deletion of your stored data.

6. Contact

For questions or data deletion requests, email: support@travelpal.app
